Executive Summary

P1 CTA note text is too low contrast

The .scheduling-note span inside the dark consultation section renders as white text over a semi-transparent light card. Measured contrast is about 3.24:1.

P2 Muted text on lavender is slightly low

Several normal paragraphs on lavender cards use --color-muted. Measured contrast is about 4.40:1, just below the common 4.5:1 readability threshold.

What works

Primary text, white text on dark CTA backgrounds, navigation text, footer links, mint surfaces, and white cards have solid contrast.
